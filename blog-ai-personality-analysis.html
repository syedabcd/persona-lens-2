How to Analyze Chat Logs with AI

Guide 5 min read

We've all been there: staring at a screenshot of a conversation, sending it to three different group chats, asking, "What did he mean by that?" or "Is she actually interested?"

The problem is that our friends are biased. They want to protect us, or they project their own experiences. Artificial Intelligence, however, is objective.

The Hidden Metadata of Text

Using Mindlyt, you can export chat logs from WhatsApp or iMessage (as .txt files) or simply upload screenshots. The AI analyzes metadata that humans often miss:

  • Response Latency: Does the time gap between messages indicate enthusiasm, business, or a power-play? Consistent delays in response to emotional topics can be a sign of avoidant tendencies.
  • Sentiment Volatility: Are they consistent, or do they oscillate between hot and cold? This "intermittent reinforcement" is addictive but often unhealthy.
  • Linguistic Matching: Do they mirror your vocabulary? High linguistic style matching (LSM) is a strong predictor of long-term compatibility and empathy.

The Big Five Model

Mindlyt maps these digital signals to the Big Five Personality Traits (OCEAN): Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism.

For example, a person who uses complex sentence structures and varied vocabulary might score high in Openness. A person who replies instantly and uses many emojis might score high in Extraversion and Agreeableness.
