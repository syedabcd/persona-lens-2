B2B Sales Psychology 101: Stop Guessing

Business 4 min read

Cold emailing is a numbers game, but closing deals is a psychology game. If you send the same generic pitch to a CEO who values innovation and a CFO who values risk mitigation, you will likely lose at least one of them.

The 4 Buying Personalities

Mindlyt's B2B mode categorizes your leads into four distinct psychological segments based on their LinkedIn profiles and email syntax. Understanding these can drastically improve your conversion rates.

1. The Driver

Brief, direct, results-oriented. They don't want fluff.

Strategy: Be concise. Focus on ROI and bottom-line impact. Do not bore them with details.

2. The Analytical

Data-driven, cautious, detail-oriented. They fear making a mistake.

Strategy: Send case studies, whitepapers, specs, and guarantees. Prove it works.

3. The Expressive

Enthusiastic, big-picture thinkers. They value innovation and relationships.

Strategy: Focus on the vision, the future, and social proof. Get them excited.

4. The Amiable

Relational, conflict-avoidant, slow to decide. They value trust.

Strategy: Build a relationship. Offer support. Do not push too hard or you will scare them off.

Tailoring your communication style to match the prospect's personality is the secret weapon of top-tier salespeople.
